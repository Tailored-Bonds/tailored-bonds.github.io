Meet Our Chairman

Lamar N. Jensen: A Legacy in Business and Finance

Lamar N. Jensen is a seasoned entrepreneur and visionary leader with over four decades of experience in global finance, business development, and strategic investments. As the Founder and Chairman of Worldwide Holdings Investment Group, LLC, Jensen has built a global empire, guiding companies through high-growth phases, market expansions, and public offerings. His expertise in identifying opportunities and driving corporate value has positioned Worldwide Holdings as a leader in industries such as oil and gas, finance, technology, and infrastructure, operating in over 135 countries.

Educational Foundation and Early Career

Jensen’s journey in business began with a degree in Business Administration from Brigham Young University. Armed with a strong foundation in finance and management, he entered the corporate world, quickly rising to leadership roles at Citigroup/Primerica, where he served as Regional Vice President and National Sales Director. In these positions, he developed a reputation for his strategic thinking, leadership skills, and ability to scale operations. His work with Citigroup laid the groundwork for his future successes, particularly in financial structuring and team development.

Entrepreneurial Ventures and Financial Acumen

Jensen’s entrepreneurial spirit came to life with the founding of Discover Mortgage Company, where he grew the business into a major player in the mortgage industry, funding over $1 billion annually. His ability to build, scale, and manage high-volume financial operations demonstrated his deep understanding of the financial services industry.

Following this, Jensen took on leadership roles at American Diversified Inc. and Royal Millennium Group Ltd (RMGL), where he further honed his expertise in corporate finance and strategic acquisitions. As CEO of RMGL, he led the company through periods of growth and successfully took a company public in 2014, where it reached a market value of $15.4 billion within a decade. His experience with public offerings and guiding companies through critical growth phases is one of the hallmarks of his business career.

Global Business Leadership at Worldwide Holdings

Since founding Worldwide Holdings in 2012, Jensen has expanded the company’s operations into 18 industries across 135 countries. His strategic vision for Worldwide Holdings has focused on acquiring high-potential companies and positioning them for exponential growth. He is currently involved in taking multiple companies public, including a project with an expected market value projected to reach $36 billion.

Jensen’s business philosophy is rooted in identifying undervalued or emerging companies, restructuring them for success, and overseeing their expansion into global markets. His extensive experience in managing oil and gas investments, energy assets, and financial portfolios has placed Worldwide Holdings at the forefront of international business development. His work across Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and North America showcases his ability to navigate the complexities of global markets while delivering substantial value to shareholders and stakeholders alike.

Expertise in Company Valuation and Growth

A key aspect of Jensen’s career has been his expertise in company valuation. Whether it’s through taking companies public, managing mergers and acquisitions, or providing strategic guidance, Jensen has proven his ability to dramatically increase the market value of the companies he oversees. His financial engineering skills have made him a trusted advisor in the business world, known for his ability to unlock hidden value and maximize returns.

Lamar N. Jensen’s extensive background in finance, strategic acquisitions, and business development makes him one of the foremost leaders in global investment management today. His ability to grow businesses, manage financial risk, and capitalize on global opportunities has defined his career. As the Chairman of Worldwide Holdings, Jensen continues to lead with vision and expertise, driving the company’s mission to create sustainable growth and value for all its partners around the world.
